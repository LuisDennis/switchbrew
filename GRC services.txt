GRC (Game Recording) uses libstagefright and the NvMMLite TVMR library for writing [[4.0.0|recorded]] video to MP4s.

Error reporting is done using the new [[NV_services#/dev/nverpt-ctrl|/dev/nverpt-ctrl]] ioctls.
