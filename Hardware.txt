== Motherboard ==
There are 3 known revisions of the motherboard:
* HAC-CPU-01 (Launch)
* HAC-CPU-10
* HAC-CPU-20 (Earliest manifacture date seen: 26/10 2017)

What actually changed between different revisions is unknown.
