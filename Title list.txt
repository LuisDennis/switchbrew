== System Modules ==
Note: Unlike 3DS, Switch doesn't have a dedicated HTTP-sysmodule. Instead, processes using HTTP(S) just use libcurl + socket/ssl services.

As of [[2.0.0]], the only sysmodule with a RomFS is [[HID_services|hid-sysmodule]].

Decimal versions use the format:
* Bit31-Bit26: Major
* Bit25-Bit20: Middle
* Bit19-Bit16: Minor
* Bit15-Bit0: Build?
